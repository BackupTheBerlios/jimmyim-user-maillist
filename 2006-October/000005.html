I think you should check your settings...:) You probably misspelled your
password or username, because it works when I try to connect with my
account. Port and server fields in the new account menu do not matter in
case of MSNP.  

We haven't tested JimmyIM on Ericsson K800i yet, so I can't tell for sure
what is the problem.

JimmyIM is currently 1.0.RC(Release Candidate), and it needs some work
