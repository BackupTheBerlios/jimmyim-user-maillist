Dear Sirs!

We have decided not to implement Yahoo protocol in this version of JimmyIM
because Yahoo constantly changes the login sequence, and it is almost
impossible for third party clients to implement Yahoo protocol. Sory for the
inconvinence, we will remove the Yahoo button from the New Account menu and
try to implement Yahoo in version 2.

JimmyIM development team would like to thank you for your interest in our
application. Please stay informed about upgrades & newest version through
our website: jimmyim.berlios.de and our mailing lists.
