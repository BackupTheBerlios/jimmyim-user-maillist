Just thought I'd let you know that I've gotten a bit further.

First I realized that I had to select the Wireless toolkit as J2ME 
Device->Group. That solved most of the build errors and made the lib ref 
to J2MELIB look much better.

Then I went ahead and installed subeclipse and imported jimmy/Trunk 
directly into Eclipse, that made things look much better. The wireless 
toolkit got selected "out of the box" for instance.
